Help
Frequently asked questions about using FitPro.
How do I track a workout? ▼
Go to Today ₒ tap any exercise to expand it → tap each set box when completed. The rest timer starts automatically between sets.
Can I change my goals? ▼
Yes! Go to Profile and update your goals anytime. Your workout plan updates immediately.
How do I change my diet (Veg / Non-Veg)? ▼
Go to Profile → Edit Profile → tap Veg or Non-Veg → Save Profile.
How do I add progress photos? ▼
Go to Body Progress ₒ tap any photo slot (Front/Back/Side/Face) → select a photo from your gallery. Photos are stored locally on your device.
How is my calorie target calculated? ▼
We use the Mifflin-St Jeor formula with your age, height, weight and gender to calculate your BMR, then multiply by your activity level.
How does the splits tracker work? ▼
There are 5 progressive levels. Complete all exercises in a level to unlock the next. Train daily — flexibility improves with consistency.
Does FitPro work offline? ▼
Yes! After the first load, FitPro works fully offline. All your data is stored locally on your device.
How do I install FitPro as an app? ▼
On Android, open FitPro in Chrome → tap the menu (three dots) → "Add to Home Screen". On iOS, open in Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen".
