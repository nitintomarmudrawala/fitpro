💪 How are workouts personalized?
Based on your BMI, gender, age, and goal, FitPro selects the right exercises, intensity, and sets for you. Underweight users get muscle-building programs. Overweight users get fat-loss + strength combos.
🥗 How is calorie target calculated?
Using Mifflin-St Jeor formula for BMR, adjusted by your activity level (TDEE). Then we add or subtract based on your goal: fat loss (-500 cal), maintenance (same), muscle gain (+300 cal).
📸 Are my photos private?
100% private. All photos are stored only on your phone (localStorage). They never leave your device. No server, no cloud upload.
⏱ How do set timers work?
Tap the set circle to mark it done. A rest timer pops up automatically. For timed exercises (plank, cardio), tap "Start Timer" to count down. A beep sounds when done.
🧘 How do I start the splits?
Go to Gym & Yoga → scroll to the Splits section. Start Level 1 daily. Hold each stretch 60 seconds minimum. Progress to next level when it feels easy. Full splits in 90-120 days with daily work.
👥 Can my friends use this too?
Yes! Share the APK. Each person registers their own username and gets a completely personalized plan based on their own BMI, gender, and goals. All data is separate.
